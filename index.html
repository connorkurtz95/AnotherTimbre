Jürg Frey & Magnus Granberg concert at the Huddersfield Contemporary Music Festival,

Sunday 26th November



Another Timbre is sponsoring a concert at this year’s Huddersfield Contemporary Music Festival. We commissioned Jürg Frey and Magnus Granberg to compose new works based on two fragments of early music - Johannes Ockeghem’s ‘Déploration sur la mort de Binchois’ and William Byrd’s ‘Oh Lord How Vain’. From this shared starting-point the composers worked independently to produce pieces for an ensemble of 10/11 players, and the new works will be premiered together at an hcmf concert on Sunday 26th November.


Performers include Another Timbre regulars such as Philip Thomas, Angharad Davies, Anton Lukoszieve, Mira Benjamin, Dominic Lash, as well as Jürg Frey and Magnus Granberg themselves.


The Festival also includes a realisation by Clara de Asis of d’incise’s ‘Appalachain Anatolia’, which we released earlier this year, and two concerts of music by Linda Catlin Smith, who is composer in residence at this year’s festival.
